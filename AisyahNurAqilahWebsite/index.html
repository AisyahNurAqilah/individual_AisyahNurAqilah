    Chicken Chop with Black Pepper Sauce

    Ingredients

    Chicken Marinade

  • 4 pieces of Skinless Chicken Thighs
    •
  • 2 tsp Soy Sauce
    •
  • 1 tbsp Ketchup
    •
  • 2 tsp Worcestershire Sauce
    •
  • 2 tsp Brown Sugar
    •

    Black Pepper Sauce

  • 1 tsp Soy Sauce
    •
  • 1/4 tsp salt
    •
  • 1 tsp Corn Starch
    •
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire Sauce
    •
  • 1 tsp Brown Sugar
    •
  • 1/2 tsp Ground Black Pepper
    •

    Chicken Chop

  • 1 tbsp Butter
    •
  • 2 tsp Olive oil
    •
  • Ketchup
    •
  • 1/2 Small onion
    •
  • 2 cloves of Garlic
    •

    STEP DIRECTIONS
    1 To make the marinade, we should mix soy sauce, worcestershire sauce,ketchup and brown sugar in a small bowl
    2 Pour marinade over the skinless Chicken Thigh and gently massage it into the chicken
    3 Let the chicken marinade in the fridge for at least 2 hours and up to overnight
    4 Grill or pan-fry the chicken over medium-high heat for about 2 minutes on each side or until fully cooked
    5 In a small bowl, mix all the ingredients of black pepper sauce.
    6 Melt Butter in a pan over medium-low heat, then add olive oil
    7 Add onion and garlic, saute until the onions are soft and translucent
    8 Add the black pepper sauce mixture, then turn up the heat. Let the sauce simmer for just a minute. This will allow it to thicken up
    9 Pour the sauce over the chicken, then serve right away. Enjoy it.
